About Me

Hello, I'm Carmen, a dedicated student and aspiring UX designer committed to transforming user challenges into seamless experiences at scale. With a background in hospitality, I've built strong communication skills, an excellent work ethic, and a positive can-do attitude. I'm empathetic, team-oriented, user-focused, and results-driven. I've successfully tackled projects ranging from commerce systems to web applications.

My journey into UX design started after earning my first college diploma in business management while helping manage my family business that is now celebrating it's 35th year anniversary. Initially geared towards taking over the family business, my time there sparked a passion for digital design. This ignited my path toward UX. This unique blend of hands-on experience and formal education equips me with a holistic perspective and a genuine passion for creating meaningful user experiences.
